ABSYZ
ABSYZ is one of the most successful pure play Salesforce consulting partner in the ecosystem. We provide a full range of professional service enabling customers to leverage Salesforce and grow their business. Our specialty lies in providing end-to-end value creation in Salesforce.com related services like Salesforce.com consulting, Implementation, Design, Appexchange development, Mobility, Integration, Testing and Staff Augmentation. We help companies elevate their business.

For those of you wondering about the origination of our name ABSYZ, it is simply derived from the Sanskrit word ‘AB’ that stands for the state of being in the Now or Present, and fused with the word ‘SYZ’ to overlay it with futuristic tones. In a dynamic and altering technological landscape, we envision ourselves as a Company that delivers solutions relevant to the present market conditions. Our name also symbolizes our capabilities in providing you with solutions from A to Z, and everything in between.

The novelty of the organization is our Service Strategy. The fact that we are a small company facilitates a faster turnaround time. Efficiently pruning out multiple layers in the delivery cycle, and ensuring most of our resources are multi tasking and techno functional developers, enables us to provide faster solutions than our competitors. We follow an agile model of expansion, and have a consistent association with our existing stakeholders based on our tenacious and timely deliverables.

We at ABSYZ boast of a dynamic and diverse team consisting of many resourceful and efficient developers and consultants spread across the Indian Subcontinent. Even though we speak various languages our uniting force and strength is our passion for Salesforce. This is further embedded by our five core values - Credibility, Integrity, Innovation, Customer Experience & Spirit of Experimentation. Along with these five values, we ensure an enriching and fulfilling experience to all our internal & external stakeholders.

With the dynamic nature of the business, we at ABSYZ are constantly redefining the consulting and outsourcing processes. As a group of professionals with valuable experience and knowledge garnered from top consulting firms globally, we have come together with a mission to simplify and streamline the consulting experience. We endeavor to deliver the simplest, quickest and most economical solutions to our stakeholders. We would like them to view us as their ongoing consulting partners who provide them with innovative, robust and scalable technology that meets not just their requirements, but also drives their business providing tangible and fruitful results. In a short span of four years we have had more than a 100 successful engagements and implementations and are constantly on the lookout to utilize our investments and resources to drive your success.
